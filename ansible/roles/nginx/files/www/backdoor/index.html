smartCAMBRIDGE Platform (the back door)

This is the technical 'backdoor' to the smartCAMBRIDGE real-time data system known as "Rita".

Here is where you can browse the real-time data (and historical archive data) relating to transport in our region. The platform is very much a work-in-progress, and will in due course serve web pages and 'apps' very much designed for the local traveller. In the meantime these pages provide a technical insight into what is going on under the covers.

If sometimes these links are not working, relax and come back later... this state-of-the-art platform is undergoing continual development, helping Cambridge become the Smart City of the Future.
