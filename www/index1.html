The Smart Cambridge team is working together with a wide range of technology businesses to provide a 'digital' platform to support the significant transport infrastructure investments taking place in our region.

We're aiming to lead the way in providing real-time digital infrastructure supporting transportation and other aspects of living in our region.

This work is being supported by the Connecting Cambridgeshire and Greater Cambridge City Deal partnerships, and this website is where the technology will emerge as it is developed.

More information about the Smart Cambridge programme can be found on the Connecting Cambridgeshire website.
